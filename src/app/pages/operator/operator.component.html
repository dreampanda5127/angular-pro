By the regulation, the Commission can not issue a Borehole Driller’s license but a “Borehole Drilling license”. The Commission is expected by law to enthrone controls over the activities of the drillers in abstracting the groundwater resources. Hence, a borehole drilling license is a regulatory certificate authorizing a proponent to access the groundwater resources and certifying a driller’s competence on the technical knowhow of harnessing groundwater in line with the Commission practice order.

The license empowers a driller to harness or carry out borehole drilling activities in any part of the state. The license is not transferable and subject to revocation. The License can be obtained by either as an individual or as a corporate body or organization, the difference would be in the approved regulatory fees and it has an expiring period when it should be renewed subject to a further regulatory inspection clearance by the Geo-Scientist

Lagos State Water Regulatory Commission (LASWARCO)
The Lagos State Water Regulations Commission (LSWRC) has implemented an online registration system for borehole drillers to obtain a license. This new system aims to streamline the registration process and ensure proper regulation of borehole drilling activities within Lagos State. By registering online through the LSWRC website, borehole drillers can now conveniently obtain the required license and comply with the state's regulations.
Step-by-step Guide:
  • 1. Visit the Lagos State Water Regulations Commission website: Open a web browser and navigate to the official website of the LSWRC (https://www.lswrc.gov.ng).
  • 2. Navigate to the Borehole Drillers Registration page: On the home page of the LSWRC website, look for the "Borehole Drillers" section, typically found in the navigation menu.
  • 3. Read the guidelines and requirements: Once you have accessed the Borehole Drillers Registration page, carefully read and understand the guidelines, requirements, and any other relevant information provided. These guidelines will provide you with a better understanding of the necessary documents, fees, and procedures involved.
  • 4. Fill out the online registration form: Click on the "Register now" or similar button to proceed with the online registration process. You will be directed to a form that requires you to input personal and business details, such as your name, contact information, company name, address, and other relevant information. Fill out all mandatory fields accurately.
  • 5. Upload required documents: The online registration form will prompt you to upload specific documents, such as your company's registration certificate, tax identification number, certificate of incorporation, and any other required supporting documents. Scan these documents in advance and ensure they meet the specified file format and size requirements.
  • 6. Pay the registration fee: Once you have completed the registration form and uploaded the necessary documents, you will be prompted to make a payment for the registration fee. The website will guide you through various payment options, such as online banking
