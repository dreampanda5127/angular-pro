DID YOU KNOW?

LASWARCO is the governing authority with full legal rights to manage and regulate drillers' and water tanker's operations and its licensing in Lagos State. We enforce regulations to ensure the provision of safe and reliable water supply across the state. By obtaining licenses from the commission, drillers and water tanker operators are assured of compliance with legal requirements and industry standards.
